# Word Embeddings

Word Embeddings convert words into numerical vectors.

## Why?

Machines cannot understand text directly.

## Popular Techniques

### Bag of Words (BoW)

Represents text using word frequency.

### TF-IDF

Measures importance of words.

### Word2Vec

Captures semantic relationships.

Example:

King - Man + Woman = Queen

### GloVe

Global Vectors for Word Representation.
