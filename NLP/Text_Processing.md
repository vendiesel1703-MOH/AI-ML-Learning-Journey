# Text Preprocessing

Text preprocessing improves text quality before model training.

## Steps

### Tokenization
Splitting text into words or sentences.

Example:
"AI is amazing"

Tokens:
["AI", "is", "amazing"]

### Stop Word Removal

Common words removed:

- is
- am
- are
- the
- a

### Stemming

Reduces words to root form.

Examples:

- Playing → Play
- Running → Run

### Lemmatization

Converts words to meaningful base form.

Examples:

- Better → Good
- Studies → Study
